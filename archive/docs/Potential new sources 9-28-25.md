## Notes

- Some sources may require RSS feed discovery (check page source for `<link rel="alternate" type="application/rss+xml">`)
- Newsletter-only sources may need email-to-RSS services like Kill the Newsletter
- Premium/paywalled sources (HBR, The Information) may have limited RSS content
- Check feed frequency before adding to avoid overwhelming daily updates
